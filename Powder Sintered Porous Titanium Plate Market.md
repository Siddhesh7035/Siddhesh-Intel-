Powder Sintered Porous Titanium Plate is an advanced material widely used in various industrial applications due to its unique properties such as high porosity, superior mechanical strength, and excellent corrosion resistance. This material is manufactured through a sintering process where titanium powder is compressed and heated below its melting point to create a highly porous and durable structure. These plates are commonly employed in industries such as medical, fuel cells, filtration, and industrial hydrogen production, where precise filtration and structural integrity are crucial.
