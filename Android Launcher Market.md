An Android launcher is a type of application that can be installed on an Android device to change the appearance and functionality of the home screen and app drawer.

Android launchers allow users to customize their device's home screen and app drawer by changing the icons, widgets, themes, and other elements. They also provide additional features and functionality, such as gesture controls, customizable app drawers, and support for multiple home screens.
