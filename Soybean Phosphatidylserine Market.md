Soybean phosphatidylserine is a naturally occurring phospholipid found in soybeans, commonly used as a dietary supplement. It plays a critical role in cell membrane integrity and function, particularly in brain cells. Known for its potential cognitive benefits, phosphatidylserine is often used to enhance memory, focus, and mental clarity. Extracted from soy or cabbage, it serves as a key ingredient in dietary supplements and functional foods.
