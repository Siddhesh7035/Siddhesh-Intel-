Collagenase is a proteolytic enzyme responsible for breaking down collagen, a primary structural protein in connective tissues. This enzyme is crucial in medical, pharmaceutical, and research applications, particularly in wound care, tissue dissociation, and dermatology. Sourced from bacterial and animal origins, collagenase is widely utilized in cell culture processes, regenerative medicine, and enzymatic debridement.
