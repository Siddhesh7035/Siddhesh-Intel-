What are Radial Agriculture Tires?
Radial agriculture tires represent a significant advancement over traditional bias-ply tires, featuring steel belts arranged perpendicularly to the direction of travel for optimized performance. These specialized tires are engineered for heavy-duty agricultural machinery including tractors, harvesters, and sprayers, offering distinct operational advantages:

Enhanced load distribution that reduces soil compaction by up to 30% compared to bias-ply alternatives
Superior traction performance through flexible sidewalls that conform to uneven terrain
Fuel savings of 8-12% due to lower rolling resistance
Extended service life with tread wear patterns lasting 20% longer than conventional tires
The technology has become particularly crucial for modern precision farming operations where minimizing soil impact and optimizing equipment efficiency directly correlate with crop yields and operational costs.
