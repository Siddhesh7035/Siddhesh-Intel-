Automotive Air-Conditioning Electric Compressors, or AC Electric Compressors, are electrically-powered air conditioning compressors used in hybrid or electric vehicles. Unlike traditional automotive air conditioning systems, which rely on the vehicle's engine to power the compressor, electric compressors use electricity from the vehicle's battery or electrical system.
