The global AR Optical Waveguide Module market was valued at US$ 53.60 million in 2023 and is anticipated to reach US$ 774.29 million by 2030, witnessing a CAGR of 47.68% during the forecast period 2024-2030.

An AR Optical Waveguide Module is a key component in augmented reality (AR) devices that enables the overlay of digital information onto the real world. It is a type of optical combiner that allows users to simultaneously view the real world and digital content displayed on the AR device.

The major global manufacturers of AR Optical Waveguide Module include Lumus, WaveOptics (Snap Inc), Raypaitech, Lochn Optics and Beijing Lingxi-AR, etc. In 2023, the world's top five vendors accounted for approximately 78.40% of the revenue.
