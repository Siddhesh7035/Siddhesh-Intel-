Silane is a silicon-based compound commonly used in various industrial applications, particularly in the semiconductor, solar, and display industries. Silicon gases such as silane (SiH₄) serve as essential materials in the production of polysilicon semiconductors and LCD displays. The compound is widely utilized in coatings, adhesives, sealants, and as a key component in the electronics industry.

Market Size
